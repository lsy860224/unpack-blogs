## TL;DR

AIGrit is a hands-on AI tool review blog. We use each tool for at least 3 days, then compare speed, cost, and accuracy with numbers. We write — we don't sell.

## Review principles

1. **Validation** — hands-on results, not ad copy. Minimum 3 days of use before any review.
2. **Numbers** — speed, cost, accuracy expressed in numbers. Test setup and sample size disclosed.
3. **Comparison** — 1:1 or N:N comparisons preferred over solo reviews. Alternatives are always included.
4. **Context** — we specify "as an office worker" / "as a developer" scenarios so readers can map their own situation.
